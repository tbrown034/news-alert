## Ukraine/Russia Conflict

### 1. Futura Doctrina - Mick Ryan
- **URL:** https://mickryan.substack.com
- **RSS:** https://mickryan.substack.com/feed
- **Focus:** Military strategy, Ukraine war, Indo-Pacific defense, future warfare
- **Author:** Retired Australian Army Major General, CSIS & Lowy Institute fellow
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts/week (including "The Big Five" weekly roundup)
- **Subscribers:** 38,000+
- **Value:** Professional military perspective with strategic analysis. Combines operational insights with broader geopolitical context. Banned in Russia for his Ukraine coverage.

### 2. Phillips's Newsletter - Phillips P. O'Brien
- **URL:** https://phillipspobrien.substack.com
- **RSS:** https://phillipspobrien.substack.com/feed
- **Focus:** Strategic studies, Ukraine-Russia war, grand strategy
- **Author:** Professor of Strategic Studies, University of St Andrews
- **Frequency:** 4-5 posts/week (Weekend Updates + analysis pieces)
- **Value:** Academic rigor applied to real-time conflict analysis. Regular podcast with Ukrainian analyst Mykola Bielieskov. Commentary in The Atlantic, The Times, MSNBC, CNN, BBC.

### 3. Comment is Freed - Lawrence Freedman
- **URL:** https://samf.substack.com
- **RSS:** https://samf.substack.com/feed
- **Focus:** War strategy, Ukraine conflict, foreign policy analysis
- **Author:** Emeritus Professor of War Studies, King's College London; UK official historian of Falklands War
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts/week
- **Subscribers:** 81,000+
- **Value:** One of the world's preeminent war studies scholars. Author of "Command: The Politics of Military Operations from Korea to Ukraine." Deep historical context for contemporary conflicts.

### 4. Ukraine Conflict Monitor - Konrad Muzyka
- **URL:** https://rochanconsulting.substack.com
- **RSS:** https://rochanconsulting.substack.com/feed
- **Focus:** Russia, Belarus, Ukraine military intelligence
- **Author:** Director of Rochan Consulting, independent defense analyst
- **Frequency:** Weekly detailed reports (30+ pages each)
- **Subscribers:** 9,500+
- **Value:** Professional-grade military intelligence from an open-source intelligence firm. Regularly advises think tanks, risk advisory firms, and international organizations.

### 5. The Counteroffensive - Tim Mak
- **URL:** https://www.counteroffensive.news
- **RSS:** https://www.counteroffensive.news/feed
- **Focus:** On-the-ground Ukraine war reporting, frontline democracy
- **Author:** War correspondent based in Kyiv, former NPR investigative correspondent, former US Army medic
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts/week
- **Subscribers:** 142,000+
- **Value:** Narrative journalism from inside Ukraine. Human stories that illustrate the war's impact. Founded in May 2023, now a team of seven in Kyiv.

## China/Taiwan/Indo-Pacific

### 6. ChinaTalk - Jordan Schneider
- **URL:** https://www.chinatalk.media
- **RSS:** https://www.chinatalk.media/feed
- **Focus:** US-China relations, technology competition, China policy
- **Author:** Senior Fellow at CNAS, former Rhodium Group and Bridgewater
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts/week
- **Subscribers:** 65,000+
- **Value:** Largest think tank Substack. Audience includes senior government officials, White House leadership, Congressional staff. Deep coverage of US-China tech competition.

### 7. China In Arms - Wendell Minnick
- **URL:** https://chinainarms.substack.com
- **RSS:** https://chinainarms.substack.com/feed
- **Focus:** China/Taiwan military modernization, defense industry
- **Author:** 35-year military journalist, former Jane's Defence Weekly, former Asia Bureau Chief for Defense News
- **Frequency:** 1-2 posts/week
- **Value:** Unmatched expertise on Taiwan defense issues. Coverage of PLA capabilities, Taiwan military readiness, cross-strait dynamics. Author of 20+ monographs on Chinese/Taiwanese weapons systems.

### 8. Sinification - Thomas des Garets Geddes
- **URL:** https://www.sinification.org
- **RSS:** https://www.sinification.org/feed
- **Focus:** Chinese establishment intellectual thought, PRC international relations
- **Author:** Tech and China journalist, RUSI associate fellow, former China Fellow at The Economist
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Value:** Unique translations and analysis of Chinese elite discourse. Praised by The Economist's former Beijing Bureau Chief as standing out "for spotting and translating the trends and intellectual currents that matter."

### 9. Pekingnology - Zichen Wang
- **URL:** https://www.pekingnology.com
- **RSS:** https://www.pekingnology.com/feed
- **Focus:** Chinese domestic policy, elite discourse, China-world relations
- **Author:** CCG Research Fellow, Princeton MPP, former Xinhua correspondent (11 years)
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts/week
- **Subscribers:** 23,000+
- **Value:** Insider perspective from former Chinese state media journalist. Ranked #2 among "10 Essential China Newsletters" by China Britain Business Council.

### 10. Sinica Podcast - Kaiser Kuo
- **URL:** https://www.sinicapodcast.com
- **RSS:** https://www.sinicapodcast.com/feed
- **Focus:** China current affairs, culture, intellectual trends
- **Author:** Kaiser Kuo, co-founder of The China Project
- **Frequency:** Weekly podcast + essays
- **Value:** Running since 2010, the flagship China podcast. In-depth conversations with scholars, authors, and policymakers. Supported by University of Wisconsin Center for East Asian Studies.

- **RSS:** https://schoolofwar.substack.com/feed

- **RSS:** https://missilematters.substack.com/feed
